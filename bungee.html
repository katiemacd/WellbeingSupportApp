UK Bungee Club

Make unforgettable memories with a 160ft Bungee Jump in Glasgow. Bungee jumping offers an adrenaline-filled experience that will last a lifetime! The 160ft Bungee Jump at Glasgow offers an extremely exciting day out, less than a 10 minute drive from Glasgow centre. As you travel the 160ft height you will enjoy views of the surrounding areas including the Glasgow Harbour. As you reach the top you will have a few seconds to prepare before hearing the famous countdown "3, 2, 1, Bungee!" and then...it's up to you!
