Flip Out

Flip Out Glasgow's arena is over 63,000 square feet and filled with 34 trampolines! Not only that, but they've got E-Karting, Interactive Football, modern Bumper Cars and an intergalactic Laser Quest arena. All of their exhilarating attractions are included within the entry price too.

Their awesome attractions include Trampolines, Ninja Wipeout, Laser Quest, E-Karting, Battle Beams, Bumper Cars, Interactive Football, Donut Slide, Slamball, Tumble Tracks, and more!
